# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 30, 2026

## Introduction

Vocaby ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

We do **not** require you to create an account, and we do **not** collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected (via Third-Party Services)

**Google AdMob** — Our advertising partner — may automatically collect certain information to serve personalized ads, including:

- Advertising ID (AAID on Android / IDFA on iOS)
- IP address
- Device type and operating system version
- Approximate location (not precise GPS)
- App usage data and ad interactions

For full details, see Google's Privacy Policy:  
[https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Locally Stored Data (Never Sent to Us)

The following data is stored **only on your device** using AsyncStorage and is never transmitted to our servers:

- Vocabulary lists and learning progress
- Spaced repetition system (SRS) review schedules
- Daily streak and goal preferences
- App settings (theme, daily goal, notification preferences)
- Word of the Day viewed status

This data can be deleted at any time via **Settings → Reset all progress** or by uninstalling the App.

## How We Use Your Information

- **AdMob**: To display relevant advertisements and measure ad performance
- **Local storage**: To provide core app functionality (learning, progress tracking, streaks)

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary for AdMob to serve ads.

## Data Retention

- **Local data**: Retained on your device until you reset progress via Settings or uninstall the App
- **AdMob data**: Governed by Google's data retention policies as described in their Privacy Policy

## Your Rights and Choices

### Opt-Out of Personalized Ads

- **iOS**: You will be prompted for App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission on first launch. You can also disable ad tracking in **Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking**
- **Android**: You can opt out of interest-based ads in **Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization**

### Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time:
1. Open the App
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Tap **Reset all progress**

Uninstalling the App also removes all locally stored data.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.
